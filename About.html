Hi! I’m Noa, a UX/UI designer originally from Sweden,
now based in the Bay Area. I specialize in creating
intuitive and visually compelling digital experiences
that prioritize user needs and ease of use. With a
background in both user research and interface design,
I focus on blending form and function to craft
engaging, effective designs.

In my portfolio, you’ll find a range of projects that
showcase my skills across the entire design
process—from early brainstorming and wire framing to
prototyping and user testing. Each project reflects
my commitment to a user-centered approach, with
iterative testing and refinement to ensure that each
interface is as intuitive and functional as it is
visually appealing. I thrive on the opportunity to
solve complex design challenges and love building
products that not only look great but also provide
a seamless, engaging experience for users.

Whether it’s a web app, mobile interface, or any
interactive platform, I’m passionate about turning
ideas into practical, beautiful designs that make
a difference.
Let’s create something amazing together!
