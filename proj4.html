Wingify

Flying made simple

The Project

The Winify app is designed to make flight booking simpler, smarter, and more affordable. It allows users to easily search for flights, compare prices across airlines, and set up automated systems to track price fluctuations. With an intuitive interface, users can set their desired price for flights and receive real-time notifications on price changes, making it easier to grab the best deals as soon as they become available. The app is crafted for travelers who want control over their budget without the hassle of constant monitoring, creating a seamless experience from search to booking.

Role: Lead Designer

Tools: Figma

2024

The Problem

One of the biggest challenges in booking flights is dealing with constantly fluctuating prices. Travelers often find themselves checking prices repeatedly, hoping for a drop that might allow them to save on their tickets. Unfortunately, flight prices are highly unpredictable, and users can either miss out on the best rates or end up overpaying by waiting too long. This uncertainty can make booking flights stressful, especially for budget-conscious travelers who are looking to maximize their savings.

The Solution

To solve this, Winify offers a unique Auto-Purchase feature that allows users to set a target price for their flight. When the ticket price drops to or below the desired amount, the app automatically purchases the ticket on the user’s behalf, ensuring they never miss a deal. If the target price is not met, the user still pays only the original ticket cost, without any increase. This system provides peace of mind and eliminates the need for constant monitoring, letting users focus on planning their trips rather than worrying about prices.

Target User

Sarah Thompson
Pain Point: Sarah finds it frustrating that flight prices are so unpredictable. She often misses out on lower prices because she doesn't have time to constantly monitor rates, and sometimes she ends up paying more than she’d like.
Goal: Sarah wants an easy way to secure the best price for flights without having to monitor fares constantly. She hopes for a solution that automates this process, allowing her to get the best deals while keeping her busy schedule on track.

Lisa Chen
Pain Point: For Lisa, keeping travel costs down is crucial, especially given the fluctuations in her freelance income. However, tracking prices for flights to different locations can be time-consuming and stressful, as she has to frequently monitor for the best rates.
Goal: Lisa wants an app that will automatically monitor flight prices for her preferred destinations, alerting her to drops and securing tickets within her budget without requiring her constant attention. She aims to travel affordably without sacrificing time that could be spent on her design work, making the process as stress-free and efficient as possible.

Brainstorming

In the brainstorming phase of this project, I focused on designing an intuitive, user-centered layout for the Winify app. I started with several initial layout ideas aimed at guiding users smoothly through core features like flight search, price tracking, and Auto-Purchase. To enhance the user experience, I explored different ways to make flight comparison quick and effortless, ensuring filtering options were accessible and easy to navigate. I also thought through the integration of features like Auto-Purchase and Price Alerts, considering how to keep these functions clear and unobtrusive so that users could set target prices and receive notifications seamlessly. Balancing aesthetics with usability was key throughout the brainstorming process, helping me build a foundation for a design that supports clarity, convenience, and a smooth user flow. This initial brainstorming laid the groundwork for a well-rounded app experience that caters to users’ needs effectively.

Information Architecture

Wireframes

I began brainstorming by discussing layout ideas I had in mind for the Winify app, focusing on creating a smooth, user-friendly experience. My goal was to design an intuitive interface that guides users seamlessly through key features, from flight search to price tracking and Auto-Purchase. For the search and comparison feature, I explored layouts that make filtering and comparing flights easy and quick. I then focused on integrating the Auto-Purchase option, ensuring users could set their target price effortlessly. Additionally, I considered Price Alerts to keep notifications visible yet unobtrusive, so users stay informed without disrupting their navigation. Throughout, I balanced visual appeal with function, choosing elements that support both clarity and ease of use. This process helped me shape a layout that feels intuitive for users like Sarah and Lisa, delivering convenience with a streamlined design.

Testing

Throughout testing, I discovered that certain aspects of the layout were confusing for users. Some menus were not as intuitive as anticipated, which made navigation feel challenging and disrupted the overall flow. To address this, I made adjustments to improve clarity and ensure that key functions were more accessible. However, several parts of the design received positive feedback and worked well as intended, reinforcing that some core elements were effective. These insights from testing allowed me to refine the layout, balancing improvements in usability with the strengths of the original design. The result was a more intuitive and user-centered experience that better meets users' needs.

Final Product

The final version of Winify is a streamlined, user-friendly app designed to make flight booking effortless and affordable. Users can quickly search, compare, and book flights across multiple airlines with clear and accessible filtering options. Key features include an Auto-Purchase function, which allows users to set a target price for flights; when prices drop to that amount, the app automatically secures the booking, saving time and stress. Additionally, Price Alerts notify users of price changes in real-time, giving them control over their budget without needing to monitor prices constantly. Enhanced labeling, intuitive menus, and thoughtful navigation ensure a smooth experience, allowing users to make informed decisions with ease. The final product offers a polished, practical tool that brings convenience, transparency, and smart savings to the flight booking process.

Teachings From the Project

This project was a valuable learning experience, providing me with real-world insights into testing and user-centered design. As the largest project I’ve tackled so far, it pushed me to expand my skills and adapt to new challenges, giving me a deeper understanding of the entire design process from concept to refinement. Working through user testing was especially enlightening—I was able to see firsthand how people interacted with my design, uncovering pain points and identifying areas for improvement. This experience taught me the importance of iterative testing and refinement, emphasizing how vital it is to make adjustments based on real feedback to enhance usability. Overall, this project helped me level up as a UX/UI designer, strengthening my ability to balance aesthetics with functionality and to design with a clear focus on the user’s needs. It was rewarding to see the app evolve into a polished product that meets real user expectations, and I look forward to applying these learnings to future projects.
